# Support

If you need help with FreedomDate, want to send feedback, or need to report a bug, contact:

freedomdateadmin@gmail.com

## In-App Support
FreedomDate includes support options in the Settings screen:
- Send Feedback
- Request Help
- Report a Bug

You can also choose to attach your current scenario file to help reproduce or review an issue.

## When Contacting Support
Please try to include:
- a short description of the issue
- what you were trying to do
- what you expected to happen
- what actually happened

If relevant, attaching the scenario from the app can make troubleshooting faster.

## Response Scope
Support is intended for app help, usability questions, and bug reporting. FreedomDate does not provide individualized financial, tax, or legal advice.
