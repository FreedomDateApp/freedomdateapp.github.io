# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

FreedomDate respects your privacy.

## Overview
FreedomDate is designed to help users model retirement scenarios, including spending, income, taxes, withdrawals, Roth conversions, and account balances over time.

## Information You Enter
You may choose to enter information such as:
- birth dates
- retirement dates
- life expectancy assumptions
- spending assumptions
- income assumptions
- Social Security assumptions
- account balances
- account ownership and contribution assumptions
- tax and strategy settings

## How Data Is Stored
FreedomDate stores scenario data locally on your device.

FreedomDate does not require account creation or login.

## Data Collection
FreedomDate does not collect personal data through an online account system.

FreedomDate does not use cloud-based scenario accounts.

## Data Sharing
FreedomDate does not sell your personal information.

FreedomDate does not share your scenario data with third parties.

## Support Emails
If you contact support from within the app, you may optionally attach your current scenario file to help troubleshoot an issue.

This attachment is only included if you choose to include it before sending the email.

Support emails are reviewed and sent through your device’s Mail app. FreedomDate does not send support emails automatically without your review.

## Third-Party Services
FreedomDate may rely on Apple services required for app distribution, device functionality, or in-app purchase infrastructure. FreedomDate does not provide cloud-hosted scenario storage.
